About Me

I'm a freelance web developer based in Milwaukee, Wisconsin, specializing in helping small businesses.

Hiring a small freelancer comes with a lot of benefits such as cost savings, transparency, and always having a human to talk things through with, instead of a customer service robot.

I've been interested in tech ever since I was old enough to know what it was. I consider myself a child of the internet. I grew up playing on Yahoo Games. I remember when Google became a household name, and then a verb. My first experience with web development came with editing my MySpace account. It wouldn't be until a long time later that I'd decide to do it for a living.
